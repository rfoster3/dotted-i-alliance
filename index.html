What We Do

Compliance services for federally funded construction

We focus on the three areas where public works projects are most often audited, and most easily derailed. We monitor, document, and resolve, so your project stays on schedule.

Labor Standards & Prevailing Wage

Davis-Bacon and prevailing wage compliance, certified payroll review and monitoring, on-site wage interviews, and underpayment resolution.

Section 3 — 24 CFR Part 75

Section 3 and Targeted Section 3 hiring and training benchmarks, contractor monitoring, and the reporting that documents good-faith effort.

Build America, Buy America

Domestic-content (BABA) compliance: contract provision review, supplier and waiver tracking, and the documentation that holds up to audit.
