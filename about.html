About Dotted i Alliance

The firm that keeps public works compliant.

A construction compliance consulting and project management firm built around one idea: federally funded projects succeed when every requirement is met, documented, and audit-ready.

Where regulation meets the job site

Dotted i Alliance is a construction compliance consulting and project management firm focused on public works and federally funded projects. For more than 25 years, our work has lived in the place where labor standards, Section 3, and domestic-content rules are either satisfied and recorded, or quietly missed until an audit finds them.

Federal funding comes with obligations that can stall a project or trigger findings, withheld payments, and stop-work orders when they are not actively managed. We translate those obligations into clear, manageable steps in the field, monitor the work as it happens, resolve issues before they become findings, and keep the documentation that proves the work was done right.

We serve public agencies, developers and owners, and general contractors across Tennessee and nationwide, as one accountable partner from groundbreaking to closeout.

How We Work

Four habits that keep projects defensible

01

Monitor in real time

We review compliance as the work happens, not after, so problems are caught early.

02

Document everything

Every requirement met is recorded, so the project is always audit-ready.

03

Educate contractors

We make the rules clear in the field so the same issue does not repeat.

04

Resolve before findings

We correct issues during the work, before they reach an auditor or a payment hold.
