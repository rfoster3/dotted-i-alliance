Winston-Salem Choice Neighborhood Initiative

Phases 2 & 3

Compliance documentation and forms for general contractors, subcontractors, and their employees on the Winston-Salem CNI project. Use this page to understand and meet the project's Section 3 (24 CFR Part 75) requirements.

Private project resource — shared by link.

Before you begin

  • Complete and submit your pre-construction forms to the general contractor before work begins.
  • Enter your Core Employee List in Elation Systems before submission of payroll #1, and do not revise it afterward.
  • A Section 3 Worker Certification Form is required for each employee listed on certified payrolls.
  • Questions? Contact us at info@dottedialliance.com or 615.749.0926.
